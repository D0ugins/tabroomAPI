General Tips:

You must use Chrome, or the Jitsi Meet android/iOS app to access the room.

You must give your browser permission to access your camera and microphone. If they're not working, click the video icon in your url bar and select "Allow":

Make sure to keep yourself muted when you're not talking!

Duplicate/ghost participants can be removed by typing anything in the chat.

If you have any issues, use the "Contact" tab to send us an email.

You'll need to make sure you have the correct domains/ports unblocked on your network. If you have issues, check the FAQ.

If you get repeatedly disconnected, check the System Status page for updates, close the tab, wait a few minutes, and try entering the room again.
